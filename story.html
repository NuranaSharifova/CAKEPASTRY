Our Story

Fresh Bakes Café was founded in 2014 and opened its doors to customers beginning of 2015. The bakery café is located at the first floor of Al Raha Mall, Abu Dhabi.

Fresh Bakes Café concept was conceived during the year 2014 when cupcakes were still a novelty in the market. The founders of Fresh bakes Café saw that there is a niche in the market for fresh and original products to be introduced, and thus the planning started. This small idea was taken one step forward through lots of research and experimentation at home.

Countless nights were spent in preparing ingredients and producing original products in order to come up with a unique concept. So many products were done for countless school events to testthe reaction of the public for such products. Encouraged by the positive feedback, it took two years and a lot of hard work for the dream to be put on paper and for the blueprints to start taking shape. The designs were done and construction of the bakery was started. The coffee shop was designed to a unique and tranquil ambiance that parallels the unique bakes that were to be produced. Everything fell into place like a puzzle.

Now after two years in the market, Fresh Bakes Café is proud to have created its own niche in a very competitive market. Fresh Bakes Café has become a well-known brand with a large customer portfolio. Our customers return back again and again encouraged by our top quality products and service. This has been coupled also with two ISO certificates that only a few bakeries in the region have acquired; the ISO 9001:2015 for Quality Management System and the ISO 22000:2005 for Food Safety Management System.
