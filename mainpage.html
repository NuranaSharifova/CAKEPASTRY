Welcome To
Fresh Bakes Cafe

Fresh Bakes Café is a unique bakery and coffee shop which caters for a wide selection of products that are 100% fresh and natural with no artificial Ingredients. Our products include many varieties of breakfast dishes, appetizers, salads, pastas, burgers and sandwiches that are sure to satisfy your palate. Our sweets and pastries are also unique and freshly baked using the finest ingredients available.

Indulge in our wide and unique range of cookies, pastries, cupcakes, cake pops, muffins, cakes, cheesecakes and other sweets. And don’t forget to book your special decorated cake for your special occasion. Our highly talented bakers and cake decorators will transform your idea into a beautiful piece of art to suit that very special moment.
